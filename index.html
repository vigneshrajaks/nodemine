our vision

Our goal is to build a sustainable rewards system for our NFT holders which is designed without the usual token dumping by whales/early investors. Our model allows us to generate growing, consistent rewards and long term value. Our NFT mint will fund the creation of a Mining Hardware farm (BTC + ETH), which will act as a low-risk foundation to build our Defi treasury from. We plan to invest in DAG, strong and other medium risk/reward projects to ensure we pass the test of time. Our long term vision is to build and sustain our treasury until it gets to a point where every holder earns a passive income.

our model

5,000 metal Ore NFTs will be available for mint. Each Ore will reveal 1 of 4 metal materials, each acting as an automatic entry to weekly giveaways. The supply: Platinum (400/5K), Gold (600/5K) , Silver (1.5K/5K) and Copper (2.5K/5K)

Upon winning the giveaway, the NFT holder will be rewarded a % of the DAYS yield from our mining farm + defi treasury airdropped in $usdc or $dai, depending on metal rarity minted:

Platinum:

100% of Mining Farm and defi treasury daily yield

Gold:

90% of the Mining Farm yield and 80% of the Defi treasury daily yield

Silver:

80% of the Mining Farm yield and 70% of Defi treasury daily yield

Copper:

70% of the Mining Farm yield and 60% of the treasury daily yield

Every week, 2 giveaways will take place and the holder will win a % of the daily yield the Mining farm + Defi treasury generates. This initial lottery model will start with 2 weekly giveaways but will adapt as our treasury’s grow, with the end goal of rewarding holders daily passive income. Monthly giveaways will take place for holders to win mining hardware and more!
